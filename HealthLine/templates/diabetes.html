Diabetes Risk Assessment

Enter your information to assess your risk for diabetes.

About Diabetes and Why You Should Test

Diabetes is a chronic health condition that affects how your body turns food into energy. Understanding your risk factors is crucial for prevention and management.

Risk factors include age, family history, weight, and physical activity levels. Early detection can help manage the condition effectively.

Meaning of the Form Terms

  • Age: Your current age in years. Risk increases with age.
  • BMI: A measure of body fat based on your height and weight.
  • Family History of Diabetes: Whether you have a family history of diabetes.
  • Blood Pressure: Your blood pressure level, which can affect your risk.
  • Cholesterol Levels: Your cholesterol levels, influencing overall health.
  • Weight: Your weight in kilograms, which can influence your risk.
  • Physical Activity Level: Your level of physical activity, affecting overall health.

Instructions

  1. Enter your age in years.
  2. Input your Body Mass Index (BMI).
  3. Select your family history of diabetes (Yes, No).
  4. Select your blood pressure (Normal, High).
  5. Select your cholesterol levels (Normal, High).
  6. Input your weight in kilograms.
  7. Select your level of physical activity from the dropdown.
  8. Click the Calculate Risk button to see your assessment.

Ensure all fields are filled out correctly before submitting for accurate results.
