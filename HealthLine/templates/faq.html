Read some frequently asked questions about our site.

HealthLine is a personalized healthcare platform that provides users with a variety of health tools, including AI-powered health consultations, a hospital vacancy checker, health risk calculators, and various other features to improve healthcare accessibility of all the it's users.

To use the Hospital Vacancy Checker, simply enter your location pincode, your city, and the category of the hospital in the search form, and the tool will display real-time availability of beds and other hospital resources in nearby facilities.

Health Risk Calculators are tools designed to assess your risk for various health conditions based on the information you provide, such as age, gender, lifestyle, and medical history. These calculators offer personalized insights and recommendations for better health management.
